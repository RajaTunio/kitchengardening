About Us

Plant Care Guide

Learn how to care for some of the most popular home & indoor plants.

Money Plant

  • Sunlight: Grows well in indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Water once a week, don’t let roots sit in water.
  • Soil: Well-drained garden soil or can grow in water.
  • Pro tip: Train it to climb or hang — looks great indoors.

Aloe Vera

  • Sunlight: Needs bright, indirect sunlight.
  • Water: Once every 2–3 weeks; allow soil to dry fully.
  • Soil: Cactus mix or sandy, well-drained soil.
  • Pro tip: Avoid overwatering — roots rot easily.

Mint

  • Sunlight: Prefers partial shade.
  • Water: Keep soil moist with regular watering.
  • Soil: Rich, well-drained soil.
  • Pro tip: Grow in pots to stop mint from spreading uncontrollably.
